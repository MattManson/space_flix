Project Brief

NASA are looking to create a Netflix-style media hub to share their vast selection of images and videos to encourage interest in space exploration.

MVP
Users should be able to:
-	Search for videos related to specific space exploration topics
-	Click on specific tags to get recommended related media
-	See NASA image of the day as banner
-	Hear space sounds

Extensions
-	Database for saving favourite videos
-	Space quiz (NPM planet!) / fact of the day
- pop-up videos instead of new pages a-la-netflix
